White Space & Clean Design

Apple

apple.com

Apple has always been know for it's sleek design and cleaness to their products. This also translates over to their website. It is so clean and so simple to use. It is soft to the eyes, yet still demands a users attention. The use of white space with the blackness of the site is fantasic. Just enough to give needed info, but not overbearing to be umcomfortable.

Visual Hierarchy

Simply Chocolate

simplychocolate.dk

I think the most important part of this page, and the key element of the visual hierarchy of it is first the white bold text with a fantastic image behind it. Using the size of font helps tell the user, hey look here first. The web page then prompts the user with half an image at the bottom to scroll. This little image hint patterns throughout the entire website.

Very Good Copy

verygoodcopy.com

This website really uses a lot of buttons and "hey look at me, and click me" type of design. It is very simple, easy to use. The call to action buttons are large enough to demand attention without unbalancing the visuals of the page.
