Target Audience

The target audience of the Williamsburg Chamber of Commerce are tourist companies and tourists themselves to grow the amount of traffic to the area.

Two Personas

Persona 1

Someone that is older and has not so distant family that may have lived in the Williamsburg area. They are familar with computers, but need a simple website in order to find the information they need.

Persona 2

Young married couple with children looking for places to enjoy themselves as a family. Food, entertainment, with a little bit of history would be their search. They would know how to use and surf a modern website.
